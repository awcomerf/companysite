Who We Are

Alamo Consulting is dedicated to crafting highly customized solutions for small businesses. We specialize in implementing technology in ways that are often overlooked, ensuring that your business can leverage tech to its fullest potential.


Our approach is client-centric, focusing on understanding the unique challenges and opportunities that each business faces. Whether it's optimizing your current systems, integrating new tools, or developing innovative strategies, our goal is to help you navigate the complex tech landscape with confidence.

Our Story

Alex Comerford founded Alamo Consulting after seeing many small businesses struggle to integrate technology effectively. He noticed how outdated websites and underutilized analytics tools prevented businesses from reaching their full potential. These recurring issues inspired Alex to create a solution that would address the unique tech challenges small businesses face.


Alamo Consulting was established to provide tailored tech solutions that help small businesses operate more efficiently. From creating engaging websites to implementing analytics for better decision-making, Alex’s goal is to empower small businesses to fully leverage technology, ensuring they can thrive in a digital landscape.
